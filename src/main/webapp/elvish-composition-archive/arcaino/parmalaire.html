Parmalairë

by Arcaino

Á pare, a parmahentar, laca ingolë ninen,
Mal álani hapya andalúmë.
An parmahentië mainolya ingolë,
Mal hapië tai rië pilë.

Book-verse

From me, O reader, learn much lore,
But keep me not too long a time.
For reading books doth well teach lore,
But keeping them doth naught but steal.
