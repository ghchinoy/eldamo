Unfinished Tales of Númenor and Middle Earth (UT)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editor) Christopher Tolkien; 1980

Various incomplete stories written by J.R.R Tolkien. Names in the main text are not included unless there is additional linguistic information. Other names are cited from the Unfinished Tales Index.

References are by page number; the first 2 digits of the decimal value are the line number, and the last 2 digits are the word position in the line.
