Parma Eldalamberon #15 (PE15)

— J.R.R. Tolkien, (editors) Arden R. Smith, Christopher Gilson, Patrick Wynne, Bill Welden; 2004

The fifteenth issue of Parma Eldalamberon. This issue includes some early name lists (p. 5-29, 59-64) and an early Qenya poem labeled Sí Qente Feanor by the editors (p. 31-40). It discusses some fragmentary evidence on the declension of Early Qenya Pronouns (p. 41-58) and a sizable Early Qenya Dictionary from the 1920s (p. 65-84). The remainder of the issue discusses Early rRunic Documents (p. 89-121).

References are by page number; the first 2 digits of the decimal value are the line number, and the last 2 digits are the word position in the line.
