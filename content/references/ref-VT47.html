Vinyar Tengwar #47 (VT47)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editor) Patrick Wynne; February 2005

The forty-seventh issue of Vinyar Tengwar. This issue has the first in a series of articles on the Eldarin Hands, Fingers and Numbers, most likely written in 1968. The article in this issue focuses on Eldarin names for hands and fingers with some discussion of related number-words. It is followed by extensive editorial notes with tangents on a variety of topics.

References are by page number; the first 2 digits of the decimal value are the line number, and the last 2 digits are the word position in the line.
