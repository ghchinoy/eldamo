Dan. [z] became [r]; [z] > [r]

The primitive voiced sibilant [z] became [r] in Danian (PE19/28), a change attested in Dan. meord < ᴹ✶mizdē (Ety/MIZD) and Dan. Eord < ᴹ✶ezdē (EtyAC/EZDĒ). A similar change occurred in Proto-Germanic (ref/@@@).
