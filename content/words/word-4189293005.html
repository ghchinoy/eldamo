S. Tengyl pn. “Signifier”

S. Tengyl, pn. “Signifier”
An Elvish name of “the significant star” mentioned some late notes by Tolkien (MR/385, note #385), probably derived from the same primitive form as its Quenya cognate Q. Tainacolli.
