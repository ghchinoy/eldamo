N. Mablothren m. “*Empty-handed”

A rejected name for Camlost “Empty-handed” appearing in the Etymologies and at least one name list from the 1930s (Etym/LEP, LR/405). It is a combination of mab “hand” and lothren “empty”, and may be a Noldorization of Ilk. Mablosgen.
