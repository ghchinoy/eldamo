N. Hebel Dúath loc. “Mountains of Shadow”

Earlier name of the Mountains of Shadow in Morder in Lord of the Rings drafts from the 1940s (WR/122, 180), a combination of hebel (“?fence”) and du(w)ath “night-shade”.

See S. Haudh-en-Ndengin for further discussion.
