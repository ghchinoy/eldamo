Dan. [ǣ] became [ea] in Ossiriandic; [ǣ] > [ea]

According to the Comparative Tables, long [ǣ] developed into the diphthong [ea] in the Ossiriandic dialect instead of becoming long [ā] as it did in East Danian (PE19/25).
