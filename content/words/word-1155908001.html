Q. Pallando m.

Q. Pallando, m.
One of the Ithryn Luin (Blue Wizards) who went to the East and South. The meaning of the name is unclear. Christopher Tolkien suggested that the initial element of his name may be derived from palan “far” (UT/401), and the final element seems to be the masculine agental suffix -ndo.
