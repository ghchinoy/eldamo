Q. Noldolantë [ñ], pn. “Fall of the Noldor”

Q. Noldolantë [ñ], pn. “Fall of the Noldor”

A lament for the sins of the Noldor at the kinslaying in Alqualondë (S/87). It is a compound of the words Noldo and lantë “fall”.
