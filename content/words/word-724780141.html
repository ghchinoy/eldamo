ᴹQ. Tumbolatsin loc.

A Quenya translation of Tumladen appearing in the Etymologies from the 1930s as a combination of tumbo “valley” and some variant of latina “cleared” (Ety/LAT).

Reference ✧ Ety/LAT

Elements

tumbo “deep valley (under or among hills, with high sides)”
latina “open, free, cleared (of land)” ✧ Ety/LAT.005 (latin(a))
