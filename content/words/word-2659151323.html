S. Torhir Ifant, m.

Author of Dorgannas Iaur (WJ/192). His first name seems to be a combination of taur² “mighty” and hîr “lord”, while his second name is simply iphant “aged” (as suggested by David Salo, GS/358).

References ✧ WJ/192.0308; WJI

Elements

taur² “vast, mighty, overwhelming, awful, huge; high, sublime”
hîr “lord, master”
^iphant “aged, having lived long, (lit.) year-full” ✧ WJ/192.0309 (Ifant)
