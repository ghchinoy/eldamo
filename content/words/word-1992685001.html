ᴱQ. Kaukaeldar coll. “False-fairies”

False Elves created by Morgoth in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/239), a combination of kauka “crooked” and the plural of Elda “Elf”, as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT1A/Kaukaeldar).
