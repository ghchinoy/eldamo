Q. Pelóri, loc. “Mountain Wall, Fencing Heights”

The Mountains of Aman surrounding the land of the Valar (S/37). This name was translated as “Mountain Wall” (PE17/26) and “fencing or defensive heights” (WJ/403). It seem to be a combination of pelo “boundary (fence)” and the plural of or(t)o “mountain”.
