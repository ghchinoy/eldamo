S. Beregar, m.

A Númenorean “of the House of Bëor” and father of Erendis (UT/177). The language and meaning of his name is unclear. His name could be derived from √BEREK “wild, fierce” (as suggested by David Salo, GS/344) or it could be related to the old Bëorian name Bereg (as indicated by Christopher Tolkien, WJ/232).
