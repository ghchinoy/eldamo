ᴱQ. Fenuqámen pn. “*Poison of Sickness”

Name of a poison in early notes (PE15/14), a combination of feno (fenu-) “poison” and the genitive of qáme “sickness”, as suggested by the editors (Smith, Gilson, Wynne, Welden).
