      Phonetic Rule Elements

      [áwa] > [oa] ✧ PE17/118 (awá > oa); WJ/367 (áua > oä)
      [ówo] > [uo] ✧ PE17/137 (owo > uo); VT48/28 (owo > uo)

      ᴹQ. stressed [áwa], [ówo] became [oa], [uo]; [áwa|ówo] > [oa|uo]
