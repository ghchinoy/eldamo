S. Arodreth, pn. “Noble Endurance”

A Sindarin name from Tolkien’s notes on “Words, Phrases and Passages from The Lord of the Rings” from the 1950s-60s (PE17/182). It is a compound of either arod “noble” and reth- “endure”, hence “Noble Endurance”.
