S. Borondir, m. “?Steadfast-man”

A soldier of Gondor who lived 500 years before the War of the Ring (UT/297). The initial element of his name may be OS. boron “steadfast” and its final element appears to be dîr¹ “-man” (as suggested by David Salo, GS/344).
