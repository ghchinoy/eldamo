S. Eryn Fuir, loc. “North Wood”

The original Sindarin name of Roh. Firien woods, appearing in a deleted section of Tolkien’s essay on “The Rivers and Beacon-hills of Gondor” (VT42/20). The name is translated “North Wood”, a combination of eryn “woods” and what appears to be a plural form fuir of for(n) “north”. This name is unique in that the noun eryn functions as a plural. Elsewhere eryn is used as a singular noun, as in Eryn Galen or Eryn Vorn, perhaps because Eryn Fuir is older than these other names.
