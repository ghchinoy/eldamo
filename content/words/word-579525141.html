ᴱQ. Haranoldor coll.

Name for the Noldor who remained in Valinor, appearing in Tolkien’s “Creatures of the Earth” from the 1910s (PE14/9). It seems to be a compound of a variant form of har(e) “near” and the plural of Noldo.
