References ✧ PE19/71, 75, 88

Related

  • Q. [ɸu] became [hu] after aspirates became voiceless spirants
  • initial [s] plus voiceless stops became voiceless spirants at the same time as aspirates became voiceless spirants
  • spirants after [r], [l] became stops after aspirates became voiceless spirants
  • velars were dentalized before [j] after aspirates became voiceless spirants
