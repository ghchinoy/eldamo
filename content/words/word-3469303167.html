ᴱQ. Fatanyu loc. “Hell”

Qenya name for G. Inthanfog “Hell” (GL/51). In might be a compound of Fâ “Lowest Air” and some form of tan(y)a “fire”, since its Gnomish equivalent contains G. Tanfa “hot air of the deep places”.
