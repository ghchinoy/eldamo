Dan. [u]

Primitive short [ŭ] seems to have survived in Danian in words like Utum < ᴹ✶Utubnu (Ety/TUB). Long [ū] also survived from Primitive Elvish in both dialects of Danian, as well as developing from the primitive diphthong [ui] (PE19/25).
