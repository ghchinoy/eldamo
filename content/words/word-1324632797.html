ᴹAQ. [j], [w] often reduced between vowels; [V̆{jw}V] > [VV]

References ✧ PE19/62-63; PE22/114

Order (03100)

After 02300 unstressed medial long vowels shortened
After 02700 aspirates became voiceless spirants
After 02900 [ɣ] between a vowel and [e] and became [j]
After 02900 [jei], [wou] became [jē], [wō]
Before ᴹQ. [ji], [wu] became [i], [u]
Before ᴹQ. intervocalic [j] diphthongized after long vowel
Before ᴹQ. [ei], [ou] generally became [ī], [ū]
Before 04000 [w] became [β] initially and between vowels
