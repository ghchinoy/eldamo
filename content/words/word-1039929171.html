ᴱN. Nebrachar poem

Elements

dir avosaith a gwaew hinar “like a wind, dark through gloomy places”
engluid eryd argenaid “the Stonefaces searched over mountains”
dir Tumledin hin Nebrachar “over Tumledin (the Smooth Valley) from Nebrachar”
yrch methail maethon magradhaid “orcs snuffling smelt out footsteps”
Damrod dir hanach dalath benn “Damrod (a hunter) through the vale down the mountain slopes”
ven Sirion gar meilien “towards (the river) Sirion went laughing”
gail Luithien heb Eglavar “Lúthien he saw, as a star from Elfland”
dir avosaith han Nebrachar “over the gloomy places, above Nebrachar”
