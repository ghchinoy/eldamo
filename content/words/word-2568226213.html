S. Gelir, m. “Merry”

Sindarin translation of the name of Sam’s son Merry, appearing in Tolkien’s unpublished epilogue to the The Lord of the Rings and in the King’s Letter (SD/126, 129). Most likely this name is an elaboration of gell “joy”.

Conceptual Development: In drafts of the epilogue, this name was initially (rejected) Riben (SD/117).
