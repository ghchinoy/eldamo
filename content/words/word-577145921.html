Q. Elostirion, loc. “*Star Fort”

The tallest of the white towers on the Emyn Beraid (S/292, UT/411 note #2). This name was probably a compound of él “star” and ostirion “fort”.

Conceptual Development: In Lord of the Rings drafts from the 1940s, ᴹQ. Elostirion was a transient replacement for Osgiliath (TI/423, WR/81).
