S. Adanel, f.

A maiden of the house of Marach who married the Bëorian Belemir (WJ/233). She was described as a “Wise-woman” (MR/305). The name of her brother Magor “The Sword” is Sindarin, so it seems likely her name is as well. If so, it is probably is a compound of Adan “man” and the feminine suffix -iel¹.
