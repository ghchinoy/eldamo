S. Lalaith, f. “Laughter”

Name of Túrin’s deceased sister, translated “Laughter” (S/198). It is apparently the noun lalaith used as a name.

Conceptual Development: When she was first mentioned in Silmarillion revisions from the 1950s-60s, her name was given as Lalaeth (MR/374).
