ᴱQ. †Taimavar m. “Shepherd of the Sky”

Older name for Taimordo appearing in the Qenya and Gnomish Lexicons from the 1910s (QL/62, 88; GL/29, 58), a compound of taime “sky” and mavar(do) “shepherd”.
