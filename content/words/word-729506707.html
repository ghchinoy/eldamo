S. Men-i-Naugrim, loc. “Dwarf Road, (lit.) Way of the Dwarves”

The road through Mirkwood, glossed “Dwarf Road” (UR/280-1) and translated more literally “Way of the Dwarves” by Christoper Tolkien (UTI/Men-i-Naugrim). It is a combination of men² “way”, i¹ “the” and Naugrim “Dwarves”.
