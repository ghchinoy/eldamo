Ilk. olg adj. “hideous, horrible”

An adjective meaning “hideous, horrible” derived from the root ᴹ√ÚLUG (Ety/ÚLUG). Its Telerin cognate ᴹT. ulga indicates a primitive form *✶ulgā, so that in the Ilkorin word the short [u] became [o] preceding the final [a] before that final vowel vanished.
