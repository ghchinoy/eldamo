Ilk. nass n. “web”

A Doriathrin noun for “web” (Ety/NAT). Its Quenya cognate ᴹQ. natse indicates a primitive form *✶natsē, where the [ts] became [ss] because medial voiceless stops became [s] before [s] in Ilkorin.
