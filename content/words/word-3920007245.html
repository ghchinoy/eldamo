Q. Luvailin, loc. “Shadowmere”

A mere (lake) in Eldamar under the shadow of Oiolossë, mentioned only in Tolkien’s “Unfinished Index” of The Lord of the Rings (RC/217). Its second element is likely ailin “lake”, and its initial element is probably some derivative of the root √LU(M)B “shadow, darkness”.
