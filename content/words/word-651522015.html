Q. Alamanyar, coll. “(Elves) Not of Aman”

An earlier version of Úmanyar, differing only in its use of the prefix al(a)-¹ for negation instead of ú- (MR/170). In some late notes from the end of the 1960s, Tolkien considered restoring this form (PE22/156).
