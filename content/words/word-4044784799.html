S. Bar-goll, loc. “Hollow Dwelling”

Another name for Nogrod (NS. Novrod), a more direct translation of Kh. Tumunzahar “Hollowbold” (WJ/414). The initial element of the name is bar “dwelling” and the second element is the lenited form goll of coll² “hollow” (WJ/414).
