ᴱQ. Tirios Otsoyáma loc. “City of Seven Names”

A name of Gondolin in a very early name list (PE13/102), a combination of tirios “city”, otso “seven” and yáma “name”. Ironically, this name brough the total up to eight.

Reference ✧ PE13/102.1401 ✧ “City of Seven Names”

Elements

tirios “town with walls and towers, burg”
otso “seven”
yáma “shout; call; name”

Cognates
