Q. Lëa-vinya, f. “Lëa the Young”

A (transient) name given for the wife of Tulkas in Silmarillion revisions from the 1950s-60s (MR/65-7), who was normally given as Nessa. The meaning of the initial element is unclear, but the second element is vinya¹ “young” (MR/67).
