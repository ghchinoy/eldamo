ᴹQ. Tengwesser coll. “Letter-names”

ᴹQ. Tengwesser coll. “Letter-names”

A term for tengwar letter names appearing in linguistic notes from the late 1930s to early 1940s, a combination of tengwe “writing” and the plural of esse¹ “name” (PE22/22, 50).
