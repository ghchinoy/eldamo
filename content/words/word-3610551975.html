Q. Vanyarin, pn. “*of the Vanyar; language of the Vanyar”

An adjective describing the the Vanya Elves, as well as a name for their dialect of Quenya (WJ/361; PE18/74), it is a combination of their tribal name with the language/adjective suffix -rin.
