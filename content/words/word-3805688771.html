ᴱQ. Maiwe (Maiwi-) f.

Another name for Vána in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s, derived from the root ᴱ√MAẎA having to do with longing (QL/60). In the Gnomish Lexicon it was given as Maiwen (GL/18).
