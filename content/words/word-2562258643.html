S. Hatholdir, m. “*Axe-man”

A man of Númenor (UT/173), his name is apparently a combination of hathol “axe” and dîr¹ “man”.

Elements

hathol “axe; broadsword-blade, axe-blade”
dîr¹ “man, adult male; agental suffix”
