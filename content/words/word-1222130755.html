S. Orcrist, pn. “Orc-cleaver”

Thorin’s sword (LotR/280), a compound of orch “Orc” and crist “cleaver”.

Conceptual Development: This name appeared in the Etymologies from the 1930s as N. Orchrist (Ety/RIS¹).
