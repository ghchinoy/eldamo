ᴱQ. Sornekiris loc. “Eagles’ Cleft”

Qenya cognate of G. Cristhorn in an early name list (PE13/105), a combination of sor(ne) “eagle” and kiris “cleft” as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT2A/Cris Ilbranteloth).
