✶Ad. k-series

A series of velar consonants in Primitive Adûnaic. Two of the sounds in this series ([ɣ] and [ʔ]) vanished by the time of Classical Adûnaic. The velar nasal [ŋ] appeared only from the assimilation of the dental nasal [n] before velar consonants (SD/432).
