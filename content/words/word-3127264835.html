ᴱQ. Tulkastor m.

Father of Vaire, at this stage, the spouse of the elf Lindo, in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/16). The meaning of the name is unclear, though it might be related to Tulkas. It was preceded by two reject forms, Turenbor and Tulkasse (LT1/22).
