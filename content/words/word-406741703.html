Ilk. †[ɣ]

The voiced velar spirant [ɣ] appeared in Old Ilkorin, but no longer appeared in Ilkorin by the time of the wars of Beleriand, having developed into [g] initially (PE19/19) and vanishing medially (PE19/23).
