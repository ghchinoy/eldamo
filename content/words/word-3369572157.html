ᴱQ. Alalminóre loc. “Land of (Many) Elms”

ᴱQ. Alalminórë loc. “Land of (Many) Elms”

A region in Tol Eressëa in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/16). It is a compound of alalme “elm” and nóre “land” (QL/29, 66).
