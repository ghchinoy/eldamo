ᴱQ. Kalimbardi coll. “goblins; Germans”

Qenya name for the German people in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/44), probably a combination of kalimbo “barbarian” and the plural mardi for mardo “dweller”. In the Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa, the word was used instead for a collective name for Goblins.
