S. Baravorn, m. “Hamfast”

A Sindarin translation of the name of Sam’s son “Hamfast”, appearing in the unfinished epilog to The Lord of the Rings (SD/121) and the King’s Letter (SD/129). The first element of the name is likely bar “home”, and the second element is probably some derivative of the root √BOR(ON) “endure”, perhaps avorn (as suggested by David Salo, GS/343).

Conceptual Development: In earlier drafts of the epilog, Hamfast was translated as N. Marthanc (SD/117). This is likely a combination of a variant of N. bâr “home” and a mutated form of N. tanc “firm” as suggested by Roman Rausch (EE/3.2).
