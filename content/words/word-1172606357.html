Q. Lindarin, pn. “*of the Lindar”

An adjective describing the third tribe of Elves, the Linda, as well as a name for their language (WJ/381), more comonly know as Telerin.

Conceptual Development: In linguistic notes from the 1930s, this referred to the language of the first tribe of Elves, consistent with the usage of ᴹQ. Linda in that period (LR/193). Later this language was called Vanyarin.
