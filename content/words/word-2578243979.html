ᴱQ. Eldaros loc. “Elfhome”

Another name for Ælfwine from the earliest Lost Tales, also appearing as Eldos and glossed “Ælfhâm” or “Elfhome” (LT2/301-2). It seems to be a compound of Elda “Elf” and (perhaps) os “house”.
