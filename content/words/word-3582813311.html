ᴹQ. Lemberin pn. “*of the Lembi”

A term for the language(s) of the Lembi from linguistic writings of the 1930s (LR/171), a compound of that name with the language suffix -rin.

Conceptual Development: This term did appear in some later writings (PM/30) but was ultimately replaced by Avarin.

See Q. Lembi for further discussion.

References ✧ LR/171; LRI/Lembian

Elements

Lembi “Those That Lingered”
*-rin “-ian, racial-adjective, language”
