S. [Ilk.] ?celon, n. “river”

      Ilk. celon n. “river”

      A noun meaning “river” developed from the primitive form ᴹ✶kelun (Ety/KEL), where the final [un] became [on].

      Conceptual Development: This word was initially marked as Noldorin.
