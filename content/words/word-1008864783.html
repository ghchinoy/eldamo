Q. Essecilmë, pn. “Name-choosing”

Q. Essecilmë, pn. “Name-choosing”

A ceremony in which a Noldorin Elf chose a new name for himself or herself (MR/214), according to his or her lámatyávë (preferences in the sounds and forms of words). It is a compound of essë¹ “name” and cilmë “choosing”.
