Q. Hirilondë, pn. “Haven-finder”

A ship built by the Númenórian prince Aldarion to explore Middle Earth (UT/192). It initial element is likely related to the verb hir- “to find”, perhaps its aorist form hiri- (with the suffixal -i not changing to -e because it is not final). The second element is londë “haven”. A more literal translation might be “*Find Haven”.
