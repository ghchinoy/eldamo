ᴱQ. I Nori Landar loc. “*The Great Lands”

Name of the lands of Middle Earth in a very early map, translated by Christopher Tolkien as “The Great Lands” (LT1/84-5), a combination of i “the”, the plural of nóre “land” and the plural of landa “wide”.
