ᴱQ. Kalmaliondo pn. “Son of Light”

A name appearing in the Early Qenya Grammar (PE14/75), which might be a variant of kalmar. It is a combination of kalma “light” and the patronymic suffix -ion, and illustrates how that suffix sometimes incorporates the pluralizer -li.

See Q. Calaquendi for further discussion.
