    Phonetic Development

    AQ. initial nasals plus voiced stops reduced to nasals ŋ- < ŋg- ✧ PE19/76 (ñg- > ñ); PE19/76 (ñgy- > ny); PE19/76 (ñgw- > ñw)
    AQ. voiced stops before nasals became nasals ŋ{mnŋ} < g{mnŋ} ✧ PE19/92 (g > ñ; before nasals); PE19/95 (g > ñ; before nasals)
    AQ. [m] assimilated to following stop, aspirate or [s] ŋ{kkʰg} < m{kkʰg} ✧ PE19/98 (mk > ñk)
    AQ. [pm], [pn] and [kn] often became [mp] and [ŋk] ŋk < kn ✧ VT42/24 (kn > nk)
    Q. [ŋ] assimilated to following [n] after [e], [a], [o] {eao}ŋn > {eao}nn ✧ PE19/96 (eŋ, aŋ, oŋ > en, an, on; before n); PE19/98 (eñn, añn, oñn > enn, ann, onn)
    Q. [ŋ] before [m], [n] vanished while lengthening preceding vowel Vŋ{mn} > V̄ø{mn} ✧ PE19/96 (iŋ, uŋ > ī̜, ų̄; before n, m); PE19/97 (eŋ, aŋ, oŋ + m > ę̄, ą̄, ǭ); PE19/98 (iñn, uñn > iʒn, uʒn‽ > ín, ún)
