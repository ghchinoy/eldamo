ᴱQ. Taimordo pn. “*Sky-warrior”

Another name for the constellation Telimektar appearing in the Qenya and Gnomish Lexicons from the 1910s (QL/62, 88; GL/29, 58), a compound of taime “sky” and mordo “warrior”.
