Dan. [ǭ] became [ua] in Ossiriandic; [ǭ] > [ua]

According to the Comparative Tables, the primitive rounded long [ǭ] (as well as [ǭ] from primitive [ā]) developed into the diphthong [ua] in the Ossiriandic dialect, instead of becoming simple long [ō] as it did in East Danian (PE19/25).

Order (03100)

Before 03400 [au] became [ō] in Ossiriandic
