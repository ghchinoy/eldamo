Ad. †[ōu]

This diphthong appears in some older Adûnaic texts, but by the time of Classical Adûnaic was reduced to [ō] (SD/424).

Reference ✧ SD/424.1109 ✧ ÔU

    Phonetic Development

    Ad. [ōu] and [ēi] became over-long [ō] and [ē] ōu > ō ✧ SD/424.1307 (ÔU > Ô)
