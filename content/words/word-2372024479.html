ᴱQ. Utumnas m.

Another name for Melko in an early name list from the 1910s (PE14/12), it also appeared as Utumnar the Gnomish Lexicon, but the text it appeared in was rejected (GL/18). It seems to be an elaboration of the name of this home, Utumna.
