ᴹQ. Arkimbele loc. “Rivendell”

A Quenya name for Imladris(t) (Rivendell) appearing only in the locative form Arkimbelesse “at Rivendell” in linguistic notes from the 1940s (PE22/125). The etymology of the name is unclear, but it probably contains imbe² “dell”.
