Ad. †[ēi]

This diphthong appears in some older Adûnaic texts, but by the time of Classical Adûnaic was reduced to [ē] (SD/424).

Reference ✧ SD/424.1202 ✧ ÊI

    Phonetic Development

    Ad. [ōu] and [ēi] became over-long [ō] and [ē] ēi > ē ✧ SD/424.1312 (ÊI > Ê)
