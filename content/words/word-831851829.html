Ad. [x] ‹kh›

The Adûnaic voiceless velar spirant was represented by “kh” in Tolkien’s writing (LotR/1114, SD/419), and was mostly derived from a primitive aspirated “k”: [kʰ] (SD/418). It is pronounced as the “ch” in German “Bach”.
