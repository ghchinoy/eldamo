Q. Vinyarion, m. “*New Son”

Birth name of Hyarmendacil II (LotR/1038, PM/199). His name seems to be a compound of vinya¹ “new” and the suffix -ion “son”.

References ✧ LotRI/Hyarmendacil II; PMI

Elements

vinya¹ “young, new”
-ion “-son, masculine patronymic”
