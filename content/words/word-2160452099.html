ᴱQ. Íverin (Íverind-) loc. “Ireland”

Elvish name for Ireland in the earliest Lost Tales (LT2/283), appearing also as Íwerin or Iverindor in the Gnomish Lexicon (GL/18). The meaning of the name is unclear.
