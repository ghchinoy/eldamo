Q. Yavannamírë, pn. “Jewel of Yavanna”

Q. Yavannamírë, pn. “Jewel of Yavanna”

A species of tree in Númenor with globed and scarlet fruits (UT/167), translated by Christopher Tolkien as “Jewel of Yavanna” (UTI/Yavannamírë). This name is a compound of Yavanna and the noun mírë “jewel”.
