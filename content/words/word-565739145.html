ᴱQ. Ungwe Fuiva f. “the Spider of Night”

Another name for Ungweliante (Ungoliant) doorkeeper in the Qenya Lexicon and Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa from the 1910s (QL/103; PME/81, 104), a combination of ungwe “spider” and fuiva “murky”.
