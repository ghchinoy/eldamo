S. Haudh-en-Nirnaeth, loc. “Mound of Tears”

Another name for Haudh-en-Ndengin, translated “Hill of Tears” (S/197). It is a combination of haudh “mound”, en¹ “of the” and nirnaeth “lamentation, tears”.
