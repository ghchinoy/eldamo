ᴱQ. Tanyasalpe pn. “Bowl of Fire”

Pool where the ship of the sun rested at night (LT1/187), a compound of tan(y)a “fire” and salpa “bowl” as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT1A/Tanyasalpë).
