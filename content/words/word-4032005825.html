ᴱQ. Ulmosan pn. “Monday”

Name of the first Monday in the Valinorian fortnight (PE14/22), a combination of the name of the god Ulmo and sana “day”.

Reference ✧ PE14/22 ✧ “Monday”

Elements

Ulmo “Valon of the Seas”
sana “day (24 hours)”
