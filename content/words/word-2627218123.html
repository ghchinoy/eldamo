ᴱQ. Timpinen (Timpinend-) m. “Fluter”

Qenya name of G. Tinfang in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/95), it is simply timpinen “fluter” used as a name. It also appeared as Timpando in the Gnomish Lexicon (GL/70).
