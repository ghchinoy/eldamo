Ilk. trum n. “shield”

A noun for “shield” derived from primitive ᴹ✶turúmbē (Ety/TER), where the unstressed vowel in the first syllable vanished to produce the favored combination [tr], and later the [mb] simplified to [m] when this cluster became final.
