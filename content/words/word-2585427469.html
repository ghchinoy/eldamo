Q. Pitya-naucor, coll. “Petty-dwarves”

Quenya name of the “Petty-dwarves” (S. Nogoth Niben) (WJ/389). It is a compound of the pitya “little” and the plural of Nauco “Dwarf”. It also appeared as Pikinaukor, containing the root √PIK “small” from which pitya is derived.
