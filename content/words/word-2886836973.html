S. Edrahil, m.

An Elf of Nargathrond who went with Finrod and Túrin on the quest to recover a Silmaril (S/170). The meaning of his name is unclear.

Conceptual Development: In Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s, his name was N. Enedrion, of equally obscure meaning (LR/300).
