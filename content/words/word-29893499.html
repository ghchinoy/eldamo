ᴱQ. Kémi f. “Earth-lady, Mother Earth”

A very early name of Yavanna, glossed “Earth-lady” (LT1/79) and “Mother Earth” (GL/18). It is derived from the root ᴱ√KEME “soil” (QL/46).

See Q. Kementári for further discussion.
