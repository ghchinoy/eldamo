N. Emerin loc. “Middle-earth”

N. Emerin, loc. “Middle-earth”

A Noldorin name for “Middle-earth” appearing in the Etymologies as a cognate of ᴹQ. Ambarenya (Ety/MBAR), apparently derived from the same primitive form *✶ambarenyā.
