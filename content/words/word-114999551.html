ᴱQ. Ilsalunte pn. “Silver Boat, Moon”

A name for the Ship of the Moon in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/192), a combination of ilsa “silver” and lunte “ship”, as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT1A/Ilsaluntë).
