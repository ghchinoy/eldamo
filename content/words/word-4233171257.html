Q. Atanatar, pn. “Father of Men”

A title given to the three houses of the Elf-friends in Beleriand: the Beorians, Hadorians and Halethians, usually appearing in the plural form Atanatari “Fathers of Men” (S/103). More properly, it was used to describe the original leaders of those houses (PM/324). The title is compound of Atan “Man” and atar¹ “father”. It was also the name of the 10th and 16th kings of Gondor (LotR/1038).
