S. pedo beth mellon, “say the word mellon”

Reference ✧ VT44/26 ✧ “say the word mellon”

Related

  • pedo beth mellon “say the word mellon” is a less ambiguous way to phrase pedo mellon a minno “speak, friend, and enter; (alternately) say ‘friend’ and enter”
