N. Moria Gate Inscription (draft)

Elements

Ennyn Durin Aran Vória “the Doors of Durin Lord of Moria”
pedo mellon a minno “speak, friend, and enter”
im Narvi hain echant “I Narvi made them”
Celebrimbor o Eregion teithant i thíw thin “Celebrimbor of Hollin drew these signs”
