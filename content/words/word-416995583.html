ᴱQ. Wendíne f.

Another name for Nielíqi in an early name list (PE14/15), probably a combination of wen(di) “maiden” and the diminutive suffix -íne, as suggested by the editors (Wynne, Gilson, Hostetter, Welden, Smith; footnote #22).
