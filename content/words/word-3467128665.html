Q. Yavannildi, coll. “Maidens of Yavanna”

Elvish women who were devotees of Yavanna and learned the secret of crafting lembas (PM/404). The initial element of this name is Yavanna and the second element is probably the plural of the feminine agental suffix -ldë².
