ᴱQ. Andonavestan pn. “Gates of Summer”

Qenya name for ᴱN. Ennyn Awest in Noldorin word lists from the 1920s (PE13/143, 160). It is a compound of ando(n) “gate” and avesta² “summer”.

References ✧ PE13/143.2203; PE13/160.0805

Elements

ando(n) “door, gate”
avesta² “summer”
