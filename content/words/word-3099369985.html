Q. Eldarion, m. “*Son of the Eldar”

The son of Aragorn and Arwen (LotR/1062). His name is a compound Eldar “Elves” and the patronymic suffix -ion “-son”.

References ✧ LotRI; PMI

Elements

Elda “Elf, (lit.) one of the Star-folk” plural
-ion “-son, masculine patronymic”
