N. Fuin Gorgoroth loc. “*Night of Deadly Fear”

Another name for Taur-na-Fuin appearing in the Etymologies from the 1930s, a combination of fuin “night” and the name Gorgoroth “Deadly Fear” (Ety/ÑGOROTH).
