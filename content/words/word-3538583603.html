Ilk. [eu], [iu] became [io]; [eu|iu] > [io]

These developments were described in the Comparative Tables (PE19/25), but there is only one attested example of each: ᴹ✶ndeuro > Dor. Dior (Ety/NDEW) and ᴹ✶tiu̯kā > Ilk. tiog (Ety/TIW).
