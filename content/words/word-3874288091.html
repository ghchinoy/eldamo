Q. Canafinwë, m. “Strong-voiced Finwë”

The father-name of Maglor. Its initial element is from cáno “commander”, appearantly intended to mean “strong-voiced” (or “commanding?”) in this context (PM/352). The final element is the name of his grandfather Finwë. The short form of this name was Cáno.
