Q. Arciryas, m. “*Noble Ship”

Younger son of the 28th king of Gondor Telumehtar (LotR/1038), his name seems to be a combination of ar(a)- “noble” and cirya¹ “ship”.

References ✧ LotRI; PMI

Elements

ar(a)- “noble, high”
cirya¹ “ship, boat; swift gliding”
