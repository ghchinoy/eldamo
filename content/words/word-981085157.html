Q. Arantar, m. “*High King”

Fifth king of Arnor, his name seems to be a compound of aran “king” and the affix tar¹ “high; lord”. As such, his name might mean “*High King”, perhaps an allusion to the claim of his house over the thrones of both Gondor and Arnor.
