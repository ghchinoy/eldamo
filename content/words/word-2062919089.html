Ilk. maig¹ n. “dough”

A noun for “dough” derived from primitive ᴹ✶mazgē (Ety/MASAG), where the primitive [z] became [i] before voiced stops, as suggested by Helge Fauskanger (AL-Ilkorin/maig).
