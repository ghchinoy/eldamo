Q. Lómëanor, loc. “Gloomyland”

Q. Lómëanor, loc. “Gloomyland”

A descriptive name of Lórien appearing in the even longer Entish description of that land: Taurelilómëa-tumbalemorna Tumbaletaurëa Lómëanor (LotR/467). The name is a combination of the elements lómëa “gloomy” and -ndor “land” (LotR/1131, Let/308).
