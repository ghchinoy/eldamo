Q. Falarombar, pn.

A rejected name for the horns of Ulmo (MR/202). Its initial element seems to be derived from the root √PHAL(AS) “foam, splash”, with its second element the plural of the word romba “horn, trumpet”. It was replaced by Ulumúri, the name that appeared in the published version of The Silmarillion.
