ᴹQ. Terendul m. “Slender and Dark”

Another name for Herendil in Tolkien’s unfinished story “The Lost Road”, translated “Slender and Dark” (LR/59). In the Etymologies, the name was glossed “slender-dark” given as a compound of teren(e) “slender” and the root ᴹ√(N)DUL, which had derivatives such as nulla “dark” (Ety/DUL, NDUL, TER).
