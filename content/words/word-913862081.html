Ilk. arn adj. “red”

A Doriathrin adjective meaning “red” derived from the root ᴹ√YAR (Ety/YAR), likely derived from primitive form such as *✶yarna [jarna]. It is a good example of how initial [j] vanished in Ilkorin.
