S. Gilbarad, m.

Father of Ivorwen and maternal grandfather of Gilraen, mother of Aragorn (PM/263). The name appears to be a combination of gil “star” and barad¹ “tower”.

gil “star; (bright) spark, silver glint”
barad¹ “tower, fortress”
