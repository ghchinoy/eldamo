ᴺQ. [Q.] sérë n. “peace; [ᴹQ.] rest, repose”

ᴺQ. [Q.] sérë, n. “peace; [ᴹQ.] rest, repose”

Related

  • sérë “peace; [ᴹQ.] rest, repose” may mean “peace from lack of activity (peace and quiet)” as opposed to “peace from lack of violence and war” which would be Q. rainë “*peace, good will”
