√GWAL root. “be stirred, excited; emotion, movement of feelings”

This root is attested in the form √WAL and √GWAL. Its only Sindarin derivative, balch, points to the √GWAL, as does the prevalence of w-forms in its Quenya derivations, since primitive ✶gw- would have remained w- for longer in Quenya (PE19/75).
