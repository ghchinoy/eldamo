S. Arthor na Forlonnas, loc. “Realm of the North-harbourage”

An old (Númenórean?) name for the region of Arnor (PE17/28). It is a combination of arthor “realm”, the preposition na² “of” and its shorter name Forlonnas “North-harbourage”.
