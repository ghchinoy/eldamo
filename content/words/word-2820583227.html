S. Gundor, m.

Youngest son of Hador (S/148). The meaning of this name is unclear, though David Salo suggested its final element might be -dor “lord” (GS/352).

Conceptual Development: The name Gundor first appeared in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s, though at this stage he was the elder son (SM/108 note #11, LR/130).
