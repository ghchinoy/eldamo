    Phonetic Development

    AQ. initial voiced stops became spirants β- < b- ✧ PE19/069.3405 (b > ƀ)
    AQ. [w] initially and between vowels became [β] β-|VβV < w-|VwV ✧ PE19/072.2411 (w > ƀ; initially or between vowels)
    AQ. medial voiced stops became spirants except after nasals -β-|mb < -b-|mb ✧ PE19/090.2003 (b > ƀ); PE19/093.2311 (b > ƀ)
    -b-|mb < -β-|mb ✧ PE19/094.1503 (b > ƀ)
    AQ. [β] became [u] before voiced continuants β{rlwj} > u{rlwj} ✧ PE19/090.2004 (ƀ > u̯; before voiced medial consonant); PE19/094.1514 (ƀ > w > u̯; before l, r, w, y)
    Q. [ɸ], [β] became [f], [v] β > v ✧ PE19/069.3412 (ƀ > v); PE19/072.2503 (ƀ > v; initially or between vowels); PE19/093.2415 (rƀ, lƀ > rv, lv)
    Van. [β], [ɣ] after [r], [l] usually became stops {rl}β > {rl}b ✧ PE19/093.2408 (rƀ, lƀ > rb, lb)
