ᴱQ. Falasquil loc. “Beach of Peace”

Tuor’s home on the coast in the earliest Lost Tales (LT2/152), translated “Beach of Peace” by Christopher Tolkien (LT2A/Falasquil). It is a compound of some form of qilde “quiet” and the plural of falasse “beach”.
