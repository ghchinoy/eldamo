ᴹQ. Pelmar loc. “Enclosed Dwelling”

Apparently another name for Middle Earth in early Silmarillion maps from the 1930s (SM/41, 255), a combination of some derivative of the root ᴹ√PEL(ES) and mar “dwelling”.
