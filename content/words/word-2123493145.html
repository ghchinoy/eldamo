AQ. initial nasals plus voiced stops reduced to nasals; [mb-|nd-|ŋg-] > [m-|n-|ŋ-]

References ✧ PE17/123; PE18/103; PE19/76

Order (04500)

After 01900 [β], [ɣ] vocalized before voiced consonants ✶ndagwē > ndau̯we > nau̯we > Q. naue PE19/94
After 03800 velars were dentalized before [j]
After 04400 initial [ŋ] vanished

Related

  • initial nasals plus voiced stops reduced to nasals would be a more straightforward rule if it occurred before dentalization, but that can’t be the case because of how initial isolated [ŋ] vanished after dentalization; velars were dentalized before [j]
