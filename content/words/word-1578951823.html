Ad. nasals

A consonants pronounced with resonance in the nasal cavitity. In Classical Adûnaic, these are mostly survivals of the Primitive Adûnaic voiced-nasals (SD/418), though the primitive velar nasal [ŋ] was altered in all cases except in the combination that produced [ŋg], spelled “ng” (SD/433 note #5).
