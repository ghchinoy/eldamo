S. Nellas, m. “?Three-leaf”

Elf-maid who befriended Túrin in his youth and taught him Sindarin (UT/76). The meaning of her name is unclear, but it might be a combination of nel- “tri” and lass “leaf” (as suggested by David Salo, GS/356).
