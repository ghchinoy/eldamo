Ilk. nîw n. “nose”

A Doriathrin noun for “nose” derived from the root ᴹ√NEÑ(WI) (Ety/LIW). It is likely an example of how [ŋg] vanished before [w] lengthening the preceding vowel, especially if the [e] first became [i] before the [ŋg], as suggested by Helge Fauskanger (AL-Doriathrin/nîw).
