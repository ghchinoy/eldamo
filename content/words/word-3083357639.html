G. Thlim Quing Ilon coll.

Name for the people of Egalmoth in an early name list (PE13/104), a combination of thlim “race”, cwing “bow” and ilon “sky”, with quing ilon “sky bow” = “rainbow” as in Cris a Teld Quing Ilon “Gully of the Rainbow Roof”.
