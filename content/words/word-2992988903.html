ᴱQ. Helinyetille f. “Eyes of Heartsease”

Another name for Nessa appearing in the Qenya Lexicon and Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa from the 1910s, also appearing as Helinyen (QL/39, PME/39), apparently a combination of helin “pansy” and some variation of yéta- “look at”, as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT1A/Nessa).
