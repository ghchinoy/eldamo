Ad. *-iyê suf. “language suffix”

A suffix used for the names of languages, appearing only in Nimriyê, the languages of the Nimir (SD/414). My best guess for the form of this suffix is *-iyê with Nimir reduced to Nimr- by the Adûnaic syncope. If the first element is instead Nimri (perhaps an adjective meaning “Elvish”), the suffix could be *-yê or even *-ê, with the y inserted as the usual glide-consonant after the final -i.
