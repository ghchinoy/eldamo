AQ. [β], [ɣ] vocalized before voiced consonants; [VβC|VɣC|Vɣj|Vɣw] > [VuC|V̄øC|Vij|Vuw]

References ✧ PE19/69, 75-76, 83, 90, 94-95; PE22/149

Order (01900)

After 01700 voiced stops became spirants except after nasals
Before Q. [ei], [ou] generally became [ī], [ū]
Before 04500 initial nasals plus voiced stops reduced to nasals ✶ndagwē > ndau̯we > nau̯we > Q. naue PE19/94

Related

  • [β], [ɣ] vocalized before voiced consonants similar to initial [ɣ] before [l], [r] sometimes became a vowel
  • Q. [ŋ] vanished before [m], [n] with compensatory lengthening vs. [β], [ɣ] vocalized before voiced consonants
  • [x] unvoiced following [j], [w] via y-fronting similar to how [β], [ɣ] vocalized before voiced consonants
