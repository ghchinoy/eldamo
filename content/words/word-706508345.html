N. Duath loc.

The earliest Elvish name of the Mountains of Shadow in Morder in Lord of the Rings drafts from the 1940s (TI/309, TI/343), it is simply du(w)ath “night-shade” used as a name.

See S. Ephel Dúath for further discussion.
