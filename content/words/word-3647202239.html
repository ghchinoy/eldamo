S. †Yssi m. “Ossë”

S. †Yssi, m. “Ossë”

Archaic Sindarin name of Q. Ossë, derived from the primitive name ✶Ossai (WJ/400). It was generally replaced by the more elaborate forms Gaerys or Yssion.
