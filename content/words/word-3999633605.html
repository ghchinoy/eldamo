ᴱQ. Aryan loc. “Shadow Place”

Another name for Mandos said to mean “Shadow Place”, appearing in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/32). There is no attested word arya meaning “shadow”, but ᴱQ. Aryanor was said to be a more direct translation of ᴱIlk. Aryador “Land of Shadow”.
