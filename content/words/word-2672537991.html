    Phonetic Development

    ᴱQ. [au] became [o] before unstressed vowel, but [aw] before stressed vowel oe < aue ✧ PE12/012.2014 (áu̯e > oe (ávo))
    ᴱQ. [au] became [o] before unstressed vowel, but [aw] before stressed vowel oa < aua ✧ PE12/012.2015 (áu̯a > oa)
    ᴱQ. [ou] became [u] before [e] and [o], but [o] before [i] and [a] oa < oua ✧ PE12/012.2113 (óu̯a > oa (ua)); PE12/013.1503 (ou̯á > oá)
    ᴱQ. vowels in hiatus reduced oa < ō̆ā̆ ✧ PE12/014.1903 (ō̆ā̆ > oa)
    ᴱQ. [oe] became [oa] oe > oa ✧ PE12/012.2012 (oe (ávo) > oa)
