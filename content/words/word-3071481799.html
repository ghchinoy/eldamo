S. Bar Erib, loc. “?Lonely House”

S. Bar Erib, loc. “?Lonely House”

A stronghold in Dor-Cúarthol (UT/153). It appears to be a combination of S. bar “house, home” and a variant form erib of the adjective ereb “lonely”, thus: “*Loneyly House”.

References ✧ UT/153; UTI/Bar Erib

Elements

bar “home, house, dwelling”
ereb? “single, alone, lonely, isolated”
