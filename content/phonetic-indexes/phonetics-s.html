Sindarin Phonetics

My analysis of the Sindarin phonology is based mainly on David Salo’s work on it and the Noldorin language in A Gateway to Sindarin (GS/31-72). I have only partly analyzed the phonology of the existing Sindarin corpus, and I have not yet compared it to the phonology of earlier Noldorin or Gnomish, nor to its real-world counterpart Welsh. The results here should only be considered a very rough draft.
